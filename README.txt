Controls:

Movement: W A S D
Missiles: G (Must Be Aiming At Target)
Zoom    : Right Click
Fire    : Left Click
Jump    : Spacebar
Climb Ladders: Look Up Ladders, Jump And Run Forward.

Upgrades; Stand On Appropriate Platform, Press E
